Freedom

Freedom to Combine

The palette gives you freedom to combine the pieces freely regardless of the style, color, or texture. Adding accessories is easy and effortless.

Freedom from Season

All our pieces are carefully chosen to enable layering. As the weather warms up you can peel off the layers to your underwear, which you can then swim in!

Freedom to Explore

All our pieces can be worn formally, informally, in the office, outdoors, and smart-casually. Our crease-free, stain-free pieces always leaves you looking styled.

Freedom to Change Size

The four-way stretch technology is very forgiving when you put on or lose a few pounds or kilo's. This means you don't have any "fat" or "thin" clothing that you keep for those changes.

Freedom to Travel

Imagine only having to pack what you actually can wear despite the weather conditions, venue, or occasion. Be prepared for office drinks, a hike in the mountains and a swim in the stream. No more wrinkles when you arrive after a long flight or hike or swim!

Freedom from your conscience

All our garments are treated with stain-resistant, water-proof technology. NO MORE warm washes, softners, and ironing. All our fabrics are sourced ethically, bio-degradable, and recyclable.
